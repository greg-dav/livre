Description

An epic novel of the violence and depravity that attended America's westward expansion, following a fourteen-year-old Tennessean known only as the kid in the 1850s and his journey into the borderlands of the American West. The book chronicles the grotesque atrocities of the Glanton gang, led by a larger-than-life figure known only as Judge Holden.
