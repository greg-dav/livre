Master Prototype

All the screens wired together with shared navigation — Library, Book Detail, the Reading Timeline, and faceted Search. Click any book in the library to open the sidecar detail. Filter a shelf by your own tags from the left rail, or click a tag on a book's detail page to jump back to the filtered library. Type in the topbar field and press Enter to escalate the quick dropdown into the full Search screen.
